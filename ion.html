I am designing and developing the Blackbox, a datalogger for capturing information about the performance of an e-Bike battery in real-time.

From rapid prototyping to custom circuit design, I have successfully instrumented a test e-bike for data collection. It takes readings and sends them to a database, where they can be analysed and interpreted. With small adjustments, any e-bike can be instrumented with the designed device.

I am designing and developing the Blackbox, a datalogger for capturing information about the performance of an e-Bike battery in real-time.

From rapid prototyping to custom circuit design, I have successfully instrumented a test e-bike for data collection. It takes readings and sends them to a database, where they can be analysed and interpreted. With small adjustments, any e-bike can be instrumented with the designed device.

My work will enable a wider product offering and easy, reliable and repeatable data collection in the future. We've built the prototype and now are developing it into a fully packaged unit.
