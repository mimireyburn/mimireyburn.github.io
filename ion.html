E-BIKE BLACKBOX

Capturing data to make micro-mobility batteries smarter, safer and more sustainable. A 6-month Innovate-UK FastStart project.

PCB Design Electronics Full-Stack Software Development

Nov 2022 - Apr 2023

The eBike Blackbox is a device that logs battery data for micro-mobility vehicles, monitoring battery performance in real time. It's an integral part of a system I designed that runs in the cloud, providing analytics, control, and insights to improve battery lifespan, health, and user safety.

The Datalogger

I started with a minimum viable product: a breadboard & a lot of electrical tape. Over the following 6 months, I delivered my first custom PCB design and manufacture. Working mostly alone enabled significant responsibility and variety in my role; some highlights include:

  • Reducing the datalogger's footprint by 50% and using only standard parts
  • Collecting data accurately and reliably in real-time.
  • Refining electronics skills rewiring an ebike and performing a team teardown of a 40V battery pack.

The Cloud, the Brain and the Web App

I designed and built a user-friendly interface, which includes real-time state-of-charge calculation, catering to fleet managers responsible for managing large vehicle fleets and maintaining customer safety.

I also delivered battery charging control via MQTT, which can be automated or manually controlled from anywhere with internet access.
